# [Brevo](https://www.brevo.com/?via=outreachly) vs Acuity for Coaches: Which Platform Should You Choose?

Managing client communications, scheduling, and email marketing can feel overwhelming when you're juggling multiple tools. Many coaches face a common challenge: finding one platform that handles bookings, automations, and client nurturing without breaking the bank. If you're torn between Brevo and Acuity Scheduling, you're not alone. Let's break down which solution works best for your coaching business.

## Understanding Your Coaching Tool Needs

Before diving into comparisons, identify what matters most to your practice. Do you need robust scheduling? Automated email sequences? CRM functionality? Integration with payment processors? Most coaches need a sweet spot between affordability and features—neither a bare-bones tool nor enterprise software with unnecessary complexity.

Both platforms address coaching business needs, but they excel in different areas. Understanding their strengths helps you make an informed decision that aligns with your workflow.

## Brevo: The Email Marketing Powerhouse

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) started as an email marketing platform and has expanded into a more complete business tool. For coaches focused on client communication and list-building, Brevo offers compelling advantages.

**Key strengths of Brevo include:**

- **Generous free tier**: Send unlimited emails to 300 contacts monthly without paying a dime
- **Affordable automation**: Build complex workflows without premium costs
- **SMS capabilities**: Reach clients via text messages for appointment reminders
- **Built-in CRM**: Manage client relationships alongside email marketing
- **Simple integration**: Connects with Zapier, WordPress, and most popular tools

Brevo shines when you're building an email list, running regular newsletters, or nurturing leads through automated sequences. Many coaches use it to follow up with past clients and deliver value through consistent communication.

**Potential limitations:**

Brevo's scheduling features are basic. If you need sophisticated appointment management with calendar syncing, you'll find Acuity more robust.

## Acuity Scheduling: The Appointment Management Expert

Acuity Scheduling (now part of Squarespace) specializes in what its name suggests: managing your calendar and client bookings. It's purpose-built for service-based professionals who need reliable scheduling.

**Key strengths of Acuity include:**

- **Advanced scheduling**: Buffer times, client confirmations, automatic reminders
- **Payment collection**: Built-in invoicing and payment processing
- **Form customization**: Detailed intake forms capture client information upfront
- **Calendar synchronization**: Prevent double-booking across multiple calendars
- **Client portal**: Clients see available times and self-manage bookings

Acuity eliminates the back-and-forth of scheduling emails. Clients book directly into your calendar, payments process automatically, and reminders reduce no-shows.

**Potential limitations:**

Email marketing in Acuity is functional but basic. If nurturing leads through sequences is important, you're better served elsewhere. Acuity also costs more—starting around $15/month for basic features.

## Head-to-Head Comparison

| Feature | Brevo | Acuity Scheduling |

| **SMS Marketing** | Yes | No |
| **Payment Processing** | No | Yes, built-in |
| **Starting Price** | Free | ~$15/month |
| **Best For** | Email nurturing, lead building | Appointment booking, service delivery |

## Which Platform Wins for Coaches?

**Choose Brevo if you:**
- Focus on content marketing and email nurturing
- Want to build a sustainable email list
- Need affordable automation and SMS reminders
- Run group programs or membership communities
- Prefer spending less on tools initially

**Choose Acuity if you:**
- Need seamless appointment scheduling
- Sell one-on-one coaching sessions
- Want clients to book independently
- Require automated payment collection
- Value calendar synchronization across devices

## The Hybrid Approach

Many successful coaches use both platforms together. Use Brevo for email marketing and nurturing, then connect Acuity for scheduling and payment processing. They integrate through Zapier, allowing you to automatically add new clients to email sequences when they book.

This combination gives you premium functionality in both areas without overpaying for unnecessary features in either tool.

## Final Recommendation

If forced to choose one tool, your decision depends on your primary pain point. **For coaches struggling with scheduling logistics and client management, Acuity Scheduling is the better investment**. The time saved on administrative work justifies the monthly cost.

However, **if your challenge is staying connected with clients and building your coaching business through consistent communication, Brevo's free tier makes it an obvious starting point**. You can launch email automation today without cost.

Consider your daily bottleneck. Is it managing your calendar, or nurturing relationships with prospects? Your answer points toward the right solution. Many coaches find the hybrid approach—using both tools together—creates the smoothest client experience and the most efficient backend operations.
